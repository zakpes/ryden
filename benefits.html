Our planet

100% recyclable with no rare heavy metals

The Ryden Dual Carbon Battery is made from Carbon Complex. This material is made of common found cotton fibre, there is no need for the rare heavy metals commonly found in leading lithium batteries.

By not using these heavy metals the Ryden Dual Carbon Battery does not produce ozone damaging chemicals, such as lithium oxide and is 100% recyclable.
