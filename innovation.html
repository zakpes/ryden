Innovation

What is carbon?

Carbon is one of the most, if not, the most accessible resources our planet has to offer. Many of us know carbon as coal, charcoal or the heavily extravagant, diamond.

What few realize is that carbon is in just about everything, from your body to the food you eat. Power Japan Plus have developed a carbon based battery that is high performing, safer, more sustainable and longer lasting. It can be 100% recycled and is competitively cheaper to manufacture than other electronic batteries.

The Game Changer. Carbon Complex

In order to power their uniquely tailored design, a carbon material with specific qualities had to be made..

From this notion birthed Carbon Complex, created from naturally grown organic cotton fiber. With this discovery, Power Japan Plus is leading the way for sustainable energy production.

The Carbon Complex material is the first of it’s kind. Previous high-performance materials have relied on rare earth resources making their production expensive and unsustainable. This technology while incredibly useful for applications like cars, is also versatile and could power many other electronic devices. It is this prospect makes Carbon Complex so exclusive.

+The Carbon Complex material is the first of it’s kind

+cotton is cost-effective, reliable and sustainable

Why cotton?

Cotton is a resource in abundance worldwide, it is easy to grow, easy to engineer with the right know-how and perfectly organic.

Previous high-performance materials have relied on rare earth resources making their production expensive, unsustainable and ecologically destructive.

Carbon Complex is the first material of its kind, it’s is cost effective, reliable, sustainable and 100% biodegradable. This versatile material could be used to power all manner of electric appliances.

Life-saving potential

Carbon Complex has been displaying some very desirable medicinal traits that are also seen in active charcoal.

Active charcoal is known for it’s absorption properties and has been effectively used to remove toxins from the body.

Power Japan Plus are exploring the options of Carbon Complex’s medical possibilities with Kyushu University Hospital.
