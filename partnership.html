Current partnership

Taisan

RACING

In 2013 Power Japan Plus put the Ryden Dual Carbon Battery into the fast lane for global recognition with the renowned company, TAISAN. The Power Japan Plus fueled TAISAN race car entered into the world famous Le Mans race, 2013.

“We have faced a number of issues with electric vehicle batteries up until now. The Ryden battery from Power Japan Plus is the solution we have been searching for.”

Yasutsune Chiba, Owner of team TAISAN

Sumitomo

METAL MINING

Sumitomo Metal Mining were Japan Power Plus’s first research partners with bench product testing of the 18650 Ryden cells. These tests were necessary to adapt the technology to meet demand in specialty energy storage markets for medical devices and satellites.

Aston Martin

racing

For the second time, Power Japan Plus will be entering the Le Mans with racing titans Aston Martin. Equipped with the latest Ryden Dual Carbon Battery, the Aston Martin car is scheduled to run in the 2016 Le Mans race.

Become a partner

Power Japan Plus are looking for high demand industries such as car manufacturers to create new partnerships. These partnerships operate under a licensing business model. This will provide technology and expertise to existing battery manufacturers to produce the dual carbon battery. This technology is constantly developing and will soon appear in other electronic application areas as well as medical and satellite technology
