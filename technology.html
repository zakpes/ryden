Technology

Carbon complex

Cotton fibre revealed itself as a malleable material and engineers discovered how to increase and decrease growth of carbon fibre crystals. This breakthrough resulted in Carbon Complex.

+A BREAKTHROUGH FOR THE WORLD FOUND IN RESOURCEABLE MATERIAL

+FASTER CHARGING IN A LONGER LASTING BATTERY

Organic electrolyte

Combining the organic electrolyte (Carbon Complex) with the dual carbon battery design allows for a unique current flow within the battery. This unique current allows positively charged lithium ions to flow to the anode and negatively charged ions flow to the cathode.

Both anode and cathode are made of Carbon Complex. This design permits two separate currents resulting in a faster charging and discharging battery that lasts longer.

+A USABLE, SUSTAINABLE ENERGY SOURCE

Temperature

Carbon Complex can heat from 0 to 800 degrees in 120 secondswith only 600 watts of energy, then back down to room temperature and is immediately cool to the touch.

The vessel

Ryden Dual Carbon Batteries can be 100% discharged with no threat of short-circuiting, extending their range even further.

Operating at 4 volts, the dual carbon battery is the most advanced battery available.
